>_ GHOSTLINE

A free, private chat room for you and a small group. No account, no company behind it, no ads.

HOW IT WORKS

Messages are encrypted right in your browser before they ever leave your device (AES-256, the same standard banks use). The relay server that carries the messages only ever sees scrambled data, never your actual text or photos.

WHAT YOU NEED

A channel name and a keyphrase. Pick your own, then share them with your group through a different app (not here). Everyone who has both can join the same room.

BE REALISTIC ABOUT THIS

This is not a replacement for Signal. It's a lightweight, open tool for casual private group chats. Don't use it for anything where your safety depends on it. If you need serious protection, use Signal.

Also: the relay can't read anything, but it can still see that a channel is active — timing, message sizes, roughly how many people. Encryption hides content, not activity. And anyone who guesses your channel name can flood it with noise (they still can't read or write messages without the keyphrase).
